TruVitals

Date July 2021
Client C&A Global
Role Senior Engineer
URL iOS
Android

Objective

Modernize health-monitoring with Truvitals. Collect data on your health and quickly share it with a medical professional using Truvitals. Make the effort to take better care of yourself and your family with the latest technology. You use your phone all the time anyway. Why not use it to keep an eye on your wellness and stay protected? With the Truvitals app, Trucheck products are here to make checking your health and preparing yourself in case of illness simpler and less stressful than ever before. With this innovative, easy-to-use app, you can quickly connect to your Bluetooth-enabled Trucheck product, get a temperature reading, and see it on your phone in an instant. The app will keep a record of past data for you to view. Save the data and successfully monitor the well-being of elderly parents, fatigued kids, and other loved ones feeling under the weather. And if any readings catch your attention, send your readings to your doctor or nurse so they are immediately aware of the issue. The Truvitals app makes the medical process faster and smoother with important information at the ready. Trucheck develops groundbreaking medical products for you to better care for yourself and your loved ones. And with the Truvitals app, all that essential health-checking data can be found in one convenient place. Trucheck, the true way to check.
